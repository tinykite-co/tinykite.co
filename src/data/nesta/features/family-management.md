# Family Management

Families change. A new baby arrives, grandparents visit for a month, the kids get their own devices. Nesta's family management makes it easy to add new members, pair new devices, and adjust your household as life evolves — all without losing a single event or breaking your calendar history.

Everything about family management is designed to be simple enough that anyone in the household can handle it. Adding a family member is three fields: name, colour, and an optional avatar. Pairing a new device is one PIN. Removing a member preserves their historical events while cleaning up future assignments. No complicated admin panels, no permissions hierarchies — just a family, together.

## Key Features

- Add new family members with a name, colour, and optional avatar
- Pair additional devices using a short numeric PIN
- View all paired devices and their last sync time
- Remove family members while preserving their event history
- Unpair devices individually without affecting other connections

## How It Works

Open Nesta's settings and tap "Family." You will see your family name at the top, followed by a list of all members with their assigned colours, and below that, a list of every paired device with its name and last sync timestamp.

To add a new member, tap "Add Member," enter their name, choose a colour from the palette, and optionally pick an avatar. They appear across every device in the household within seconds. To pair a new device, tap "Pair Device" to generate a fresh PIN. Enter that PIN on the new device and it joins your family instantly.

If someone leaves the household or you need to retire an old tablet, you can remove members or unpair devices individually. When a member is removed, their past events remain on the timeline with their colour intact — nothing is lost. Only future event assignments are cleared. It is a thoughtful approach that respects both the past and the present.
