# Night Mode — Calm in the Dark

When the house is quiet and the lights are off, the last thing you want is a bright screen jolting you awake. Night mode transforms Nesta into a minimal, true-black clock display with a warm amber glow — designed specifically for OLED screens where black pixels are truly off. It is the gentlest possible way to check the time and tomorrow's first event without disturbing anyone.

Night mode is not just "dark theme." It is a completely different interface, stripped down to only what matters at 2am: the time, displayed large and warm, with tomorrow's first scheduled item shown in a whisper-soft font below. Nothing else. No timeline, no dots, no colours. Just calm in the dark.

## Key Features

- True black background for OLED screens — pixels are completely off
- Large warm-amber clock that is easy to read without being harsh
- Tomorrow's first event displayed in a soft, low-contrast font
- Automatic activation based on a configurable bedtime schedule
- Tap anywhere to briefly show tonight's remaining events

## How It Works

Night mode activates automatically at your configured bedtime — say, 9:30pm — and deactivates at your morning time. When it kicks in, the screen fades to true black and a large analogue or digital clock appears in warm amber tones. Below the clock, a single line shows tomorrow's first event: "7:15am — School drop-off."

If you wake in the night and want to see more, tap anywhere on the screen. A brief overlay fades in showing any remaining events for tonight and the first few items for tomorrow, then fades back to the minimal clock after ten seconds of inactivity.

You can also trigger night mode manually from any view by tapping the moon icon in the header. This is useful when you want the bedside clock experience outside of your scheduled hours — during an afternoon nap, for instance, or while the baby is sleeping.
