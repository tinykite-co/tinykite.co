# Family Setup in Under 3 Minutes

Getting your family onto Nesta is remarkably fast. There are no accounts to create, no emails to verify, no passwords to remember. You open the app, name your family, add your people, and you are done. The whole process takes less time than writing a grocery list.

Nesta asks for exactly what it needs and nothing more. Pick a family name, choose a colour for each member, and share a simple PIN with the other devices in your household. Every phone and tablet that enters that PIN joins your family instantly. No cloud, no sign-up forms, no "check your inbox" — just your family, connected.

::video[https://cdn.tinykite.co/nesta/videos/demo_setup_flow.mp4]

## Key Features

- Create a family with a name and optional icon in seconds
- Add family members with names and personalised colour assignments
- Pair any device in your home using a short numeric PIN
- No email addresses, no accounts, no passwords required
- Works entirely over your local WiFi network

## How It Works

When you first open Nesta, you are greeted with a single question: "What should we call your family?" Type in a name — the Hendersons, for instance — and tap next. From there, you add each family member one by one. Give them a name and pick a colour. That colour becomes their signature throughout the app: their events, their completions, their initials on the timeline.

Once your family is set up on the first device, Nesta shows a pairing PIN. Open the app on any other device in your home, tap "Join a family," and enter the PIN. The devices find each other over WiFi and sync immediately. Every device now shares the same calendar, the same family, the same beautiful paper-on-the-wall schedule.
