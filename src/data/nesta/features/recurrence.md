# Smart Recurrence

Family life runs on rhythms. Swimming every Tuesday, bin night every Thursday, piano lessons on the first and third Saturday. Nesta's recurrence picker makes these patterns effortless to set up with a visual interface that shows you exactly what you are creating before you commit.

Most calendar apps hide recurrence behind a cryptic dropdown: "Repeat: Custom..." followed by a confusing matrix of options. Nesta replaces that with a picker you can actually read. Choose your pattern, and a live preview shows the next five occurrences right there on screen. You will never wonder "wait, does this include the 14th?" again.

::video[https://cdn.tinykite.co/nesta/videos/demo_recurrence.mp4]

## Key Features

- Visual recurrence picker with plain-language pattern descriptions
- Live preview showing the next five scheduled occurrences
- Common patterns available as one-tap presets (daily, weekly, fortnightly)
- Custom patterns for complex schedules (e.g., every second Wednesday)
- Edit or stop recurrence from any single occurrence going forward

## How It Works

When creating or editing an event, tap the recurrence option to open the pattern picker. At the top, you will see quick presets: every day, every week, every fortnight, every month. Tap one and the preview below instantly updates to show the next five dates this event will appear.

For more complex patterns, tap "Custom" to build your own. Choose a frequency, pick specific days of the week, or set an interval. As you adjust each parameter, the preview updates in real time. "Every 2 weeks on Tuesday and Thursday" is not something you have to calculate in your head — you can see the dates laid out plainly, like entries in a diary.

Once you are happy with the pattern, tap save. The recurring events populate your calendar immediately, each one linked so that future edits can apply to the whole series or just a single occurrence.
