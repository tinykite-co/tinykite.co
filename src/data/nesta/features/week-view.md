# Week at a Glance

Sometimes you need to look beyond today. The week view gives your family a seven-day perspective in a compact, horizontal strip that fits naturally at the top of the screen. Each day shows small completion dots — a quick visual pulse of how the week is shaping up without drowning you in detail.

The week view is designed for the Sunday evening planning moment, or the Wednesday morning "what is coming up?" check. It is not trying to show you everything. It is showing you just enough to feel oriented, with the confidence that you can tap any day to dive deeper.

::video[https://cdn.tinykite.co/nesta/videos/demo_week_view.mp4]

## Key Features

- Seven-day horizontal strip with today highlighted
- Completion dots for each day — green for done, open for pending
- Tap any day to jump straight to its full day view
- Swipe the strip to navigate to previous or upcoming weeks
- Family member filter to see one person's week at a time

## How It Works

The week view displays seven days in a clean horizontal row. Each day cell shows the date in serif type, a count of scheduled items, and small coloured dots representing completion status. A filled dot means the item is done; an open circle means it is still pending. At a glance, you can tell whether Thursday is packed or wide open.

Tap any day to transition smoothly into the full day view for that date. The animation is subtle — the day cell expands into the timeline, maintaining visual continuity so you always know where you are. Swipe the week strip left to peek at next week, or right to review the week just gone.

To focus on a single family member, tap their colour dot in the header. The view filters to show only their events and completions, which is especially useful when helping the kids plan their week ahead.
