# Day View — Your Family's Timeline

The day view is the heart of Nesta. It is what greets you first thing in the morning, what the kids glance at on their way to school, and what you check one last time before bed. A single, clean vertical timeline that shows everything your family has happening today — grouped by category, not crammed into hourly slots.

Families do not think in 30-minute calendar blocks. They think in moments: "before school," "after lunch," "bedtime." Nesta's day view reflects that reality. Events are organised into meaningful groups — school, meals, activities, tasks — so you can scan the whole day in seconds, the way you would glance at a paper schedule pinned to the kitchen wall.

## Key Features

- Vertical timeline grouped by category with elegant serif date headers
- Past items automatically fade to a softer opacity, keeping focus on what is next
- Auto-scrolls to the current time when you open the app
- Per-member colour coding so you can instantly see who has what
- Swipe left or right to navigate between days

## How It Works

When you open Nesta, the day view is front and centre. At the top, today's date is displayed in large serif type — like a date on a wall calendar. Below that, your family's day unfolds in categorised sections: school events first, then meals, activities, and tasks.

Each item shows its time, title, and the coloured dot of the family member it belongs to. Completed items gently fade and drift to the bottom of their group. The whole screen breathes with quiet motion — nothing flashy, just a calm sense of where the day stands.

Tap any event to see its full details. Long-press a category header to collapse or expand it. Pull down to sync the latest updates from other family devices. It is simple, deliberate, and exactly what a family schedule should feel like.
