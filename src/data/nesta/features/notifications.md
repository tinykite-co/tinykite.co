# Gentle Notifications

Nesta's notifications are designed to be helpful without being intrusive. A gentle nudge before school pick-up, a morning summary of the day ahead, and complete silence during the hours you need it most. Every notification is configurable per family member, because Dad might want a 15-minute heads-up while Mum prefers 30.

Most apps treat notifications as a megaphone. Nesta treats them as a quiet tap on the shoulder. The default settings are deliberately conservative — you will get a morning summary and event reminders, nothing more. No promotional messages, no "you have not opened the app in a while" guilt trips, no noise. Just the right information at the right time.

## Key Features

- Per-event reminders with configurable lead times (5, 15, 30, or 60 minutes)
- Daily morning summary delivered at your chosen time
- Quiet hours that silence everything between configurable times
- Per-member notification preferences — each person controls their own
- Evening preview of tomorrow's schedule, if you want it

## How It Works

Each family member configures their own notification preferences from the settings screen. The options are straightforward: choose a default reminder lead time for events, set a morning summary time, and optionally enable an evening preview of the next day.

Quiet hours are the simplest setting in the app. Pick a start time and an end time, and Nesta goes completely silent between them. No reminders, no summaries, no sounds. This is especially useful for children's devices that should not be buzzing after bedtime, or for parents who want uninterrupted wind-down time in the evening.

The morning summary arrives as a single notification at your chosen time — say, 7:00am. It lists the day's events in order, colour-coded by family member, so you can scan the whole day before your feet hit the floor. It is like someone reading tomorrow's schedule off the kitchen wall for you.
