Nesta is a private messenger scoped to one household — no public feed, no strangers, no ads. Grown-ups hold the keys, and children join with a parent-issued QR, link, or code, never needing an email or a password. Inside, it's a familiar messenger for the people you live with: group and direct chats, photos, videos, files and voice notes, emoji reactions, replies, typing indicators, edit and delete, and animated stickers — with notifications that say who wrote, never what. Available on Google Play (internal), iOS TestFlight, and as an installable web app at nesta.tinykite.co.
