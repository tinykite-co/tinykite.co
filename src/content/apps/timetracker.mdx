## Overview

TimeTracker helps you understand where your time goes. Track work sessions, analyze productivity patterns, and generate detailed reports for clients or personal insights.

## Features

- **One-Click Tracking**: Start and stop timers with a single click
- **Project Management**: Organize time entries by project and task
- **Detailed Reports**: Generate comprehensive time reports
- **Privacy Focused**: All data stored locally on your device
- **Export Options**: Export data in CSV or PDF format

## Benefits

- Understand your productivity patterns
- Bill clients accurately
- Identify time-wasting activities
- Improve time management skills

## Who It's For

- Freelancers tracking billable hours
- Remote workers managing multiple projects
- Anyone wanting to understand their time usage
- Teams collaborating on projects

Start tracking your time today and gain valuable insights into your productivity.
