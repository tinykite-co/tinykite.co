## Overview

QuickNote is designed for speed and simplicity. Capture your thoughts, ideas, and reminders in seconds without any unnecessary complexity.

## Features

- **Lightning Fast**: Opens instantly and saves automatically
- **Clean Interface**: Distraction-free writing environment
- **Markdown Support**: Format your notes with simple Markdown syntax
- **Local First**: Your notes stay on your device by default
- **Dark Mode**: Easy on the eyes in any lighting condition

## Perfect For

- Quick thoughts and ideas
- Meeting notes
- To-do lists
- Code snippets
- Daily journaling

## Getting Started

Simply visit the app and start typing. Your notes are saved automatically as you write. Use Markdown formatting to organize your content and make it more readable.
